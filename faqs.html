What is REΛCH?

REΛCH is an app that helps individuals and people in groups set, track, and achieve their financial goals.

How does REΛCH work?

To use REΛCH, simply continue living your financial life and Kudi will automatically sort your expenses into their respective categories.

Who is Kudi?

Kudi is your personal financial assistant. She helps you to set your budget, track, and categorize your expenses.

What questions can I ask Kudi?

Kudi is our A.I chat bot programmed to answer all your questions about how the REACH app works to show you all your categorised expenses.

Do I need my card or bank details for my expenses to be categorized?

No. Kudi is able to track your expenses by waving her e-magic wand. She does not require your card or bank details for expense categorization.

How safe and secure is the REΛCH app?

Your security is important to us and our system is designed to protect your account. We do not access your delicate information such as account numbers, pins or passwords. We use SMS data and our advanced algorithms to automatically provide rich insights of your expenses.

What operating system does the app work with?

REΛCH works with the Android OS to track and categorize your expenses.

Why can't I use the savings function on the app?

We are overhauling this section of the app. Please watch out for our next update - soon come!

Can I manually change transactions from one category to another?

Yes. In the UNLIKELY event that Kudi incorrectly categorizes an expense, you can change the transaction to the right category.
